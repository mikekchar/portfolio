Status

I haven't updated this blog in a really long time and it is starting to show its age. I always intended this to be a kind of bliki as opposed to a normal blog. The idea is that I would update the contents as my ideas changed. Now, 5 years later, having not updated the site at all, I find some of my writing embarrassing :-). I should have things cleaned up soon. I will also have a lot of new content. It's not that I haven't been writing for 5 years -- I just haven't been posting. Feel free to browse and send me email for any comments you may have.
